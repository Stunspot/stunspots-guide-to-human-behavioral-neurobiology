## Public Positioning

**The Cognitive Ecology of Adaptive Regulation.** A model-facing canon for interpreting human behavior as embodied, predictive, metabolically constrained, socially regulated, culturally patterned, and ecologically situated.

This repository is a model-facing Markdown knowledge canon for AI/RAG ingestion, long-context reasoning, project knowledge bases, and source-traceable behavioral interpretation. It is human-readable, but its primary design target is AI systems that need a stable reasoning substrate for behavior under embodied, metabolic, social, cultural, digital, and systemic constraints.
