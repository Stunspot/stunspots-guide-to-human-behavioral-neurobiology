# Canon Map — Stunspot's Guide to Human Behavioral Neurobiology

**The Cognitive Ecology of Adaptive Regulation**  
*A model-facing canon for interpreting human behavior as embodied, predictive, metabolically constrained, socially regulated, culturally patterned, and ecologically situated.*

This canon treats human behavior as a nested regulatory ecology: embodied prediction, energy budgeting, chemical tuning, social regulation, cultural scaffolding, platform-shaped attention, nonlinear feedback, and diagnostic restraint. The report sequence is designed as a dependency chain. Later reports reuse the earlier vocabulary rather than redefining it.

## Interpretive Through-Line

The sequence moves from **ontology → evidence → prediction → energy → tuning → state dynamics → ecology → social regulation → culture → digital capture → nonlinear breakdown → diagnostic guardrails**.

That order matters. Reports H-J are not free-floating social theory. They inherit the energy, prediction, and evidence constraints of A-G. Reports K-L are not generic self-help or diagnosis manuals. They are guardrails for interpreting behavior without mistaking a repeated state, constrained policy, platform loop, or structural pressure for a permanent trait.
