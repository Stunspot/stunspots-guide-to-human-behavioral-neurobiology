## Corpus Maturity

The corpus is usable as a first public canon release. The source reports are the canonical individual units. Compiled packs and the omnibus are convenience formats for AI/RAG upload, local indexing, and long-context workflows.

This release is intentionally model-facing: it prioritizes stable terminology, source-to-output mapping, report sequence clarity, directory transparency, and retrieval usefulness over consumer-product smoothness.

## Use Boundaries

This canon is for education, research, design, reference, AI/RAG augmentation, and systems-level behavioral interpretation. It is not a medical device, clinical diagnostic protocol, therapeutic substitute, or individualized treatment guide.
